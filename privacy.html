Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Message Assistant

Important: LinkedIn Message Assistant does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. The extension operates entirely locally in your browser.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. LinkedIn Message Assistant operates as a browser extension that processes data locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect any personal data
  • Store any user information
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Access your LinkedIn messages or profile data

2. How the Extension Works

LinkedIn Message Assistant is a productivity tool that helps you compose and manage LinkedIn messages. The extension:

  • Runs entirely within your browser
  • Processes text locally on your device
  • Does not require any external API calls for core functionality
  • Does not store any of your messages or data

3. Data Usage and Storage

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to store, process, or share. Any temporary data used by the extension (such as message drafts) is stored locally in your browser and is not accessible to us.

4. Third-Party Services

LinkedIn Message Assistant does not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your personal data. The extension operates independently and locally.

5. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no security concerns regarding data breaches or unauthorized access to your information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, this is not applicable to our service.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your data as it remains on your device.
