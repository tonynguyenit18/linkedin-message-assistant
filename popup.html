How to use:

  1. Choose your preferred AI provider (OpenAI or Gemini)
  2. Enter your API key for the selected provider
  3. Select your preferred AI model
  4. Save the settings
  5. Go to LinkedIn and open a conversation
  6. Click the "AI Response" button next to the message box
